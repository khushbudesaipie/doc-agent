Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Short version: Doc Agentic only accesses your Google account to create new documents in your Drive. We do not read, store, or share your existing files or personal data.

1. About Doc Agentic

Doc Agentic ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered document generation application. Its purpose is to generate formatted Google Docs from plain-English briefs provided by users. Contact: support@docagentic.app

2. What this application does

Doc Agentic allows users to:

  • Sign in with their Google account
  • Enter a plain-English description of a document they want created
  • Receive a fully formatted Google Doc created in their own Google Drive

The application uses artificial intelligence to interpret the brief, structure the content, and write the document. It then uses the Google Docs and Google Drive APIs to create the document in the user's Drive.

3. Google OAuth scopes requested

Doc Agentic requests the following Google permissions:

  • Google Docs API — to write content and formatting into newly created documents.
  • Google Drive (drive.file scope only) — to create new Google Doc files in your Drive. The drive.file scope limits access to only files that Doc Agentic itself creates. We cannot read, access, or modify any existing files.
  • Email address (openid / userinfo.email) — to display which Google account is signed in. Not stored beyond the session.

4. Data we collect

  • Session token — stored in your browser temporarily to keep you logged in during your visit.
  • OAuth access token — stored in server memory during your session to make API calls. Deleted on logout.
  • Document brief — sent to an AI model (OpenRouter) to generate content. Not permanently stored.

We do not permanently store your name, email, Drive contents, or document content.

5. Third-party services

  • Google APIs — to create documents in your Drive.
  • OpenRouter (openrouter.ai) — AI model routing to process your brief. See OpenRouter Privacy Policy.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties except the services listed above that are required to operate the application.

7. Your rights

  • Revoke access at any time: Google Account Permissions
  • Log out at any time using the Sign out button — clears all session data immediately
  • Delete documents created by Doc Agentic directly from your Google Drive
  • Contact us: support@docagentic.app

8. Security

All connections use HTTPS encryption. OAuth tokens are never logged or written to disk.

9. Children

Doc Agentic is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

10. Changes

We may update this policy and will update the date above when we do.
