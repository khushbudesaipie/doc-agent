Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Short version: Digipie only accesses your Google account to create documents in your Drive. We do not read, store, or share your existing files or personal data.

1. Who we are

Digipie ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered document generation tool that creates formatted Google Docs from natural-language briefs. The application is operated by Digipie and can be reached at support@digipie.app.

2. What this app does

Digipie is a Google Doc Generator Agent. Its sole purpose is to:

  • Accept a plain-English document brief from the user
  • Use an AI language model to structure and write the document content
  • Create a new, formatted Google Doc in the user's own Google Drive
  • Return a direct link to that document

3. Google account access and OAuth scopes

When you sign in with Google, Digipie requests the following permissions:

  • Google Docs (create and edit) — used to write content and formatting into the newly created document.
  • Google Drive (create files only) — used to create a new Google Doc file in your Drive. We use the drive.file scope, which only allows access to files that Digipie itself creates. We cannot read, access, or modify any of your existing Drive files.
  • Email address (read-only) — used only to display your email address in the app interface so you know which account is signed in. We do not store your email address beyond your active session.

We request only the minimum permissions necessary to provide the service.

4. Data we collect and store

Digipie collects and stores as little data as possible:

  • Session data: A temporary session token is stored in your browser to keep you logged in during your visit. This expires when you log out or close the browser.
  • OAuth tokens: Your Google OAuth access token is stored temporarily in server memory during your session to make API calls on your behalf. It is deleted when you log out.
  • Document briefs: The brief you enter is sent to an AI language model (via OpenRouter) to generate document content. We do not permanently store your briefs.
  • We do not store: Your name, email address, Google Drive contents, document content, or any personal information beyond the active session.

5. Third-party services

Digipie uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google APIs (docs.googleapis.com, drive.googleapis.com) — to create and format Google Docs in your Drive.
  • OpenRouter (openrouter.ai) — an AI model routing service used to process your brief and generate document content. Your brief text is sent to OpenRouter for this purpose. See OpenRouter's privacy policy.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties except as described in Section 5 (the third-party services required to operate the app). We do not use your data for advertising.

7. Data retention

We do not retain any user data beyond the active session. When you log out, all session data and OAuth tokens are deleted from our servers immediately.

8. Your rights

You can:

  • Revoke access at any time by visiting Google Account Permissions and removing Digipie.
  • Log out at any time using the Sign out button in the app, which clears all session data immediately.
  • Delete documents created by Digipie directly from your Google Drive at any time.
  • Contact us at support@digipie.app with any privacy questions or requests.

9. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data during your session, including encrypted HTTPS connections and secure session management. OAuth tokens are never logged or written to disk.

10. Children's privacy

Digipie is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when we do. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy questions, concerns, or requests, please contact us at:
support@digipie.app
