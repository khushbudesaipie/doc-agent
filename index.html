What is Doc Agentic?

Doc Agentic is an AI document generation application. Users provide a plain-English description of the document they need — a proposal, report, scope of work, or any other document — and Doc Agentic uses artificial intelligence to structure the content, write professional copy, and create a fully formatted Google Doc delivered directly to the user's Google Drive.

The application connects to your Google account solely to create new documents on your behalf. Doc Agentic does not read, access, or modify any of your existing files.
